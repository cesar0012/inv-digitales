Dress Code

What to Wear

Please dress in a way that feels comfortable, polished, and appropriate for the celebration.

Suggested attire: semi-formal, cocktail, themed, or event-appropriate clothing based on the style of the celebration.

Guests are welcome to use the event colors as inspiration. Comfortable shoes are recommended if the celebration includes dancing, outdoor areas, or extended standing time.

“Come ready to celebrate, feel your best, and enjoy every moment.”

Dress Code

What to Wear

Please use this guide to choose an outfit that feels elegant, comfortable, and aligned with the celebration.

M

For Men

Suits, dress shirts, slacks, jackets, or polished semi-formal attire are recommended.

Colors You Can Wear

  • Black
  • Navy
  • Gray
  • Beige

Colors to Avoid

  • White
  • Ivory
  • Bright Red
  • Neon
  • A jacket or dress shirt is recommended for formal events.
  • Please avoid overly casual clothing unless the event theme allows it.
  • Comfortable dress shoes are recommended.
W

For Women

Dresses, jumpsuits, skirts, elegant separates, or polished semi-formal attire are recommended.

Colors You Can Wear

  • Sage
  • Champagne
  • Dusty Blue
  • Mauve

Colors to Avoid

  • White
  • Ivory
  • Bright Red
  • Neon
  • Please avoid wearing colors reserved for the main honoree or host.
  • Comfortable shoes are recommended if the event includes dancing.
  • Accessories may follow the event palette or theme.

Colors and attire can be adjusted by event type, theme, cultural tradition, or family preference.
